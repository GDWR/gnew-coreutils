==============
GNEW coreutils
==============


GNEW coreutils is a reimplementation of the  `GNU coreutils <https://www.gnu.org/software/coreutils>`_
in a range of languages for fun and learning.

.. caution::
    I would strongly advise to use these tools for fun and learning only.
